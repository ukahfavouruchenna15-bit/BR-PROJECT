About

BOB ARCHITECTS designs the buildings Lagos lives and works in. Founded on the conviction that architecture should be unapologetic, not polite, we build for clients who refuse to settle for the safe and the standard, whether the brief is a private residence in Lekki or a commercial tower rising over Victoria Island. We work across both worlds because we believe the line between them is artificial. A home should function with the discipline of a workplace, and a workplace should feel as considered as a home. From concept to completion, every line we draw answers to two questions: does it work, and does it command attention. Lagos is a city that moves fast and builds faster. BOBOLA exists to make sure what gets built here lasts, looks like it belongs to this decade, and gives our clients something to be proud to put their name on.
